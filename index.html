About me

Hi, I’m Vicky! As a licensed esthetician with 10+ years of experience, I’ve dedicated my career to helping people feel confident and radiant in their own skin. My passion for beauty and skincare goes beyond treatments—I love educating my clients and creating personalized experiences that leave them glowing inside and out.

Beyond my work, I’m also a proud mother of three. Balancing motherhood with my passion for esthetics has been an incredible journey, and it has taught me the importance of self-care and feeling your best in every stage of life.

At Beauty Glam by Vicky, my goal is to provide a welcoming space where you can relax, recharge, and walk away feeling beautiful. Whether you’re here for a facial, skincare advice, or a glamorous makeover, I promise to bring expertise, care, and a personal touch to every service.

I can’t wait to help you look and feel your best!
